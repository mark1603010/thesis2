About

“Traveling – it leaves you speechless, then turns you into a storyteller” – Ibn Battuta

The MUSEO Tu’ Agusan is part of the EduTourism program of Agusan del Sur State College of Agriculture and Technology, it is an online museum that serves as a virtual guide for local tourists in exploring the different attractions found in the province of Agusan del Sur.

Its purpose is to help YOU appreciate and discover the unseen beauty of Agusan del Sur. Your beautiful journey to Agusan, begins now! It is just a click away.
